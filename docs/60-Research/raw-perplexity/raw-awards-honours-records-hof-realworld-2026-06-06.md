# Raw capture — Real-world football taxonomy (Perplexity, 2026-06-06)

Verbatim capture of the Perplexity strand requesting a faithful real-world
taxonomy of awards, honours, records and Hall-of-Fame / legend recognition for a
football management game. **Structure only** — the game uses IP-safe fictional names
(GD-0015 / ADR-0007). Synthesis in [[../awards-honours-records-hof-owner-2026-06-06]].

## Response (captured) — game-ready taxonomy

### 1. HONOURS (team)
**Axes:** Scope = Domestic / Continental / Intercontinental-Global · Type = League /
Knockout cup / League cup / Super cup / Play-offs-promotion / Other · Tier = Major /
Secondary / Minor.

- **Domestic:** top-tier league titles (major); lower-division titles (secondary/minor,
  often counted as "promotions"); primary national cup (major); league cup
  (secondary); super cup / charity shield (secondary/minor); regional cups (minor);
  promotions & play-off wins (often a separate "Promotions" section).
- **Continental & intercontinental:** top-tier continental club cup (major);
  secondary continental (high but below top); continental super cup (secondary);
  intercontinental / club-world titles (major global).
- **Presentation (clubs):** "Major Honours" (domestic league, national cup, top
  continental, world) then "Other Honours" (league cups, super cups, secondary
  continental, regional, promotions). Grouped by competition → count → list of
  seasons.
- **Presentation (individuals):** grouped by club and/or national team, then by
  competition; only major honours prominent; managers may split "as manager" vs "as
  player".

### 2. RECORDS
Top categories: **Club**, **Competition/League**, **Player career**, **Manager** —
each split All-time / Single-season / Single-match / Streak.

- **Club:** most appearances; all-time & league top scorer; most clean sheets; biggest
  win / heaviest defeat; longest unbeaten/winning/winless/scoring/clean-sheet runs;
  most points/wins/goals in a season; record home/away/average attendance; record
  signing / sale; youngest/oldest player & scorer; fastest goal; most goals in a match.
- **Competition/League:** team season records (points, wins, goals for/against,
  unbeaten run); team match records (biggest win, highest-scoring game); individual
  season records (golden boot, assists, clean sheets, appearances); all-time individual
  (top scorer, assists, appearances, clean sheets); age/milestone (youngest/oldest,
  fastest goal, consecutive titles, consecutive seasons in league).
- **Player career:** career totals (apps, league apps, goals, assists, clean sheets);
  seasonal bests; international caps/goals/tournament goals; combined milestones
  (career trophies, hat-tricks); age-related (debut/retirement age, youngest to X).
- **Manager:** games managed (total / per competition); wins/draws/losses; win %;
  trophies (major vs minor; club & competition breakdown); tenure (longest spell in
  days/matches; multiple spells); season records (highest points, longest unbeaten,
  most goals); career scope (clubs managed, years, club vs international).

### 3. INDIVIDUAL AWARDS
**Axes:** Scope = club-internal / domestic-league / continental / global · Type =
performance / positional / statistical / selection / manager / tournament · Frequency
= annual / monthly / per-match / per-tournament.
- Club-internal: Player/Young-Player/Goal of the Season, Most Improved, Academy PotS,
  internal top scorer, Player of the Month.
- Domestic league: PotS/Year, Young Player, Goalkeeper, positional (DEF/MID/FWD),
  Manager of the Season, top scorer/golden boot, playmaker/assists, Best XI / Team of
  the Season, Player/Manager of the Month.
- Continental: Player/Young-Player/Goalkeeper/Coach of the Year, Team of the Year, top
  scorer, competition MVP.
- Global: World Player/Young-Player/Goalkeeper/Coach of the Year, Global Best XI,
  positional, tournament golden boot/glove, Player of the Tournament, Man of the Match.

### 4. HALL OF FAME / LEGENDS
- **Eligibility:** waiting period after retirement (e.g. 3–5 yrs); minimum career
  length/seasons; occasional special early induction.
- **Inductee categories:** player, manager/coach, referee/official, builder/contributor,
  era/pioneer.
- **Scope:** club / league / national / international HoF.
- **Induction mechanisms:** expert panel; mixed panel + (advisory) fan vote; official
  committee; OR automatic statistical thresholds (X apps, Y goals, Z major trophies,
  awards won). Process: nomination → balloting → minimum vote % → induction class.
- **HoF-level vs good career:** sustained excellence across many seasons; major-comp
  impact; major trophies/awards; among the best in position/era; iconic moments.
- **"Legend" vs HoF:** HoF = formal/institutional; "Legend" = narrative/emotional, tied
  to iconic moments, one-club loyalty, era symbolism, fan affection, cultural impact.
  Model legend as an **overlay** on HoF-level careers with club-loyalty / iconic-event /
  fan-sentiment criteria.

### 5. MANAGER prestige / legacy factors
- Quantitative: trophies (domestic/cup/continental/global), consistency (multi-season,
  multi-club, multi-country), records (points, unbeaten, doubles/trebles).
- Longevity & loyalty: long tenure; building multiple squad generations; return spells.
- Overperformance vs resources: smaller club to titles/Europe; budget-vs-results;
  giant-killing.
- Club-building: youth pipeline; recruitment/integration; institutional/playing-identity
  legacy.
- Tactical / era-defining: style innovation; manager trees; era dominance; rivalries.
- Signature achievements: invincible season, doubles/trebles, record margins, dramatic
  comebacks, first-ever/drought-ending titles.
- **Prestige archetypes:** Domestic Legend · Continental Giant · Club Icon · Tactical
  Innovator · Builder → blend into a composite legacy score (solid → successful → great
  → club legend → HoF / all-time great).
