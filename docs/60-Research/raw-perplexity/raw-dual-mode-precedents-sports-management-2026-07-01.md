# RAW — Dual-mode precedents in sports-management games

Raw capture for the dual-mode/parity research packet (EASY "stove" mode vs PRO
mode on one simulation core). Queries were run 2026-07-01 via Perplexity (Sonar)
and Exa web search. Perplexity answers are condensed to the substantive,
URL-backed parts; claims whose URLs could not be independently confirmed are
marked. Exa queries were used to verify the load-bearing claims against primary
sources.

## Query 1 (Perplexity) — Football Manager Touch history and discontinuation

Prompt: FM Touch relative to full FM (cut features), platforms over the years,
why SI discontinued Touch on PC/mobile after FM22, any adoption data, reception.

Substantive answer (condensed):

- Touch is officially positioned as a "streamlined" FM with fewer features;
  SI's own comparison page ranks depth full FM → Console → Touch → Mobile.
  Source: <https://www.footballmanager.com/compare-games>
- Touch removed/simplified media and interaction systems (much lighter team
  talks — essentially happy/unhappy), streamlined transfers, and offered
  **Instant Result** to skip match management. Sources:
  <https://www.footballmanager.com/compare-games>;
  TouchArcade FM24 multi-platform review
  <https://toucharcade.com/2023/11/14/football-manager-2024-review-touch-vs-mobile-vs-ps5-vs-pc-steam-deck-features-save-controller-console/>
- Platforms over time: PC/Mac + iPad/Android tablets (2015–FM21), Nintendo
  Switch (from FM Touch 2018), Apple Arcade (FM23 Touch onward: iPhone, iPad,
  Mac, Apple TV). Sources: TouchArcade (above); RadioTimes
  <https://www.radiotimes.com/technology/gaming/fm24-mobile-touch-netflix-apple/>;
  FM Scout FM26 comparison <https://www.fmscout.com/a-fm26-game-comparison.html>
- Perplexity could NOT produce a primary quote with Touch's sales percentage;
  it stated SI never published SKU-level splits. (Confirmed by Query 3: the
  official statement says sales "fallen significantly" without numbers.)
- Reception: Touch consistently reviewed as the "sweet spot" between Mobile
  and full FM (TouchArcade, above); hardcore players missed full press
  conference / interaction depth.
- Unverified-source caveat: Perplexity cited two YouTube reviews and a Facebook
  group thread for the team-talk simplification details; treated as
  medium/low-confidence colour, not load-bearing.

## Query 2 (Perplexity) — NBA 2K MyGM/MyLeague, FIFA/EA FC career sim tiers, Motorsport Manager

Substantive answer (condensed):

- NBA 2K MyLeague/MyNBA automation menus: per-team General automation (draft
  combine, pre-draft workouts, prospect scouting, staff management),
  Lineup automation (starters, minutes), Roster automation (free agency,
  trades, extensions, options) — each Manual or Auto. SimCast lets you watch a
  sim and jump in at moments. MyGM = narrative/RPG layer; MyNBA = "limitless
  GM experience" with sliders. NBA 2K26 has Ranked vs Unranked MyGM (unranked
  allows player editing, custom rosters, sliders). Sources:
  <https://nba.2k.com/2k26/courtside-report/mynba/>;
  <https://www.espn.com/gaming/story/_/id/46087339/nba-2k26-wnba-mynba-mywnba-mygm>;
  <https://nba2k.fandom.com/wiki/MyLEAGUE>; plus YouTube tutorials
  (low confidence, not load-bearing).
- FIFA/EA FC career: three-way match launcher — Play Match / Sim Match
  (Interactive Match Sim: 2D/top-down, subs + tactics mid-sim, jump in/out) /
  Quick Sim (instant result). Community view: quick sim more volatile than
  playing; interactive sim praised as the middle ground. Sources:
  <https://www.eurogamer.net/fifa-21-career-mode-how-to-play-sim-or-interactive-match>
  (not independently re-fetched); EA official sources confirmed in Query 5.
- Motorsport Manager: mobile line simplified (abstract car stats, simple
  sponsor deals, short sessions) vs PC line (per-part development, deeper
  finances, per-driver race commands); console edition an adaptation of PC
  with streamlined UI. Perplexity's specific URLs for the console edition
  (sega.co.uk newsitem, godisageek review) and Pocket Gamer MM3 review could
  not be verified in this session → console-simplification details kept at
  LOW confidence. PC/mobile split independently confirmed in Query 6.

## Query 3 (Exa) — SI official statement on discontinuing FM Touch

Query: "Sports Interactive official announcement Football Manager 2023 no FM
Touch on PC and tablets discontinued reason statement"

Key hits:

- **Primary source** — Sports Interactive, "Major Changes for FM22 Touch and
  Beyond" (Miles Jacobson studio statement),
  <https://www.footballmanager.com/news/major-changes-fm22-touch-and-beyond>:
  - Touch history: FM Classic standalone 2014 (PS Vita) → tablets 2015 →
    renamed Touch → standalone release from 2016/17.
  - "Initially this was very successful, but with the release of the game on
    other platforms – such as the Switch version in 2018 and then last year's
    Xbox Edition, both of which are closely modelled on Touch – and changes to
    the way people play games, **sales on the PC, Mac and tablet versions of
    the game have fallen significantly**. Developing for the tablet market,
    particularly on Android, is also very challenging, due to the huge range
    of different hardware versions available."
  - FM Touch 2022 = Switch only; no Steam/Epic/App Store/Google Play version.
  - "Removing games from the FM line-up is not something we do lightly …
    it's only ever to improve things for the majority of our players."
- FM Scout summary (2021-10-07),
  <https://www.fmscout.com/a-fm22-touch-only-on-nintendo-switch.html>:
  decision framed as a **cost-benefit analysis** (dev/QA/release cost per SKU
  vs revenue).
- SI, "Football Manager 2023 Touch returns to iOS through Apple Arcade",
  <https://www.footballmanager.com/news/football-manager-2023-touch-returns-ios-through-apple-arcade>:
  Touch returned as Apple Arcade exclusive (iPhone/iPad/Apple TV/Mac).
- SI, "The Future of Football Manager",
  <https://www.footballmanager.com/news/future-football-manager>:
  "Before we embraced these new types of platforms, we were getting close to
  2 million players a year. With FM23 … 5m players"; Apple Arcade Touch and
  PS5 Console "exceeded all expectations".
- Wikipedia, <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Football_Manager>: FM25 cancelled
  February 2025 after delays (context for the Unity/FM26 reboot).

## Query 4 (Exa) — Anstoss coarse tactic model ("the stove")

Query (German): "Anstoss 1 Anstoss 2 Taktik Bildschirm einfache Taktik Sturm
Abwehr Spieler Anzahl Einsatz Härte Regler Fussballmanager Retrospektive"

Key hits:

- Anstoss-Online Wiki, "Taktik",
  <https://wiki.anstoss-online.de/index.php/Taktik>:
  - **Ausrichtung**: 7 steps from *Abwehrriegel* to *Brechstange* (how
    offensively/defensively the team plays).
  - **Einsatz**: 5 steps from *kein* to *sehr hoher Einsatz*; higher effort
    burns more freshness ("Frische") — an explicit resource cost.
  - **Härte**: 5 steps from *Nonnenhockey* to *brutal* (tackling aggression).
  - Conditional in-match rules via the "Taktik-Tisch": tie a tactic change to
    conditions (score, goal difference, number of own players, minute).
- Anstoss-Online forum guide "#5 Aufstellung und Taktik",
  <https://forum.anstoss-online.de/index.php?article%2F24-guides-tipps-5-aufstellung-und-taktik%2F=>:
  same three-dial model as the start tactic + condition matrix.
- Anstoss-Jünger, "Tipps und Tricks - A2G",
  <https://www.anstoss-juenger.de/index.php/topic,2879.0.html> (citing the
  official TippsTricks booklet p.77): numeric trade-offs of the coarse dials,
  e.g. *Abwehrriegel* = defence +22% of attack value / attack −40%;
  *Brechstange* = mirror image; *Normal* has no penalty → community meta:
  "Normal" is generally net-optimal; *gewalttätig* ≈ +3% performance but red
  cards scale with referee strictness; *Nonnenhockey* ≈ −5%.
- Anstoss-Jünger, "Aufstellung & Taktik",
  <https://www.anstoss-juenger.de/index.php/topic,1135.0.html>: shows the
  co-existing per-player layer in Anstoss 3 — free player placement with
  **Aktionsbereiche** (action areas), per-player Passspiel (pass risk),
  Positionstreue (positional discipline), pressing, offside trap.
- angryflo.de Anstoss 1 strategy page,
  <https://www.angryflo.de/strat-anstoss.htm>: Anstoss 1 match model driven by
  summed line strengths (defence/midfield/attack) — formation = how many
  players per line ("3 up front, 5 in defence" style); counter-picking the
  opponent's strongest line is the core skill.
- Wikipedia DE, <https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anstoss_(Computerspiel)>:
  contemporary review quote praising that "bei aller Komplexität der
  Spielspaß und die Bedienung nicht auf der Strecke blieben" (complexity
  without unusable UI); ~260k copies sold in Europe (Anstoss 1).

## Query 5 (Exa) — FIFA 21 Interactive Match Sim (primary sources)

Query: "FIFA 21 career mode interactive match sim jump in jump out sim to
result Eurogamer new feature announcement"

Key hits:

- **Primary** — EA, "Pitch Notes - FIFA 21 Career Mode Deep Dive",
  <https://www.ea.com/news/pitch-notes-fifa-21-career-mode-deep-dive>:
  match launcher = "Play Match – load into traditional gameplay. Sim Match –
  launch the new Interactive Match Sim. Quick Sim – instantly jump to the
  final result of the match."
- EA, "Getting Started in FIFA 21 Career Mode",
  <https://www.ea.com/news/fifa-21-career-mode-getting-started>: Interactive
  Match Sim = bird's-eye view, subs + tactical changes during the match,
  "jump in and out of gameplay when you want to change the course of the
  game".
- PlayStation Blog, <https://blog.playstation.com/2020/08/13/5-ways-fifa-21s-career-mode-changes-the-game-for-the-better/>:
  jump in "at any point … as many times as you want"; the game "will even
  suggest opt-ins at key moments, such as when a goal is scored".
- Game Informer preview,
  <https://gameinformer.com/preview/2020/08/13/everything-you-need-to-know-about-fifa-21s-retooled-career-mode>:
  "A lot of players sim matches in career mode … you don't have to feel
  completely helpless on the sidelines"; sim runs at 2x speed with stamina,
  ratings, stats and gameplan visible.
- Gfinity, <https://www.gfinityesports.com/article/fifa-21-career-mode-reveal-all-new-features-interactive-match-sim-position-conversion-active-training-player-sharpness-more>:
  out-of-play moments auto-skipped in the sim view.

## Query 6 (Exa) — NBA 2K MyNBA/MyGM split + Motorsport Manager mobile vs PC

Queries: "NBA 2K MyNBA MyGM difference sandbox customization versus narrative
locked mode criticism"; "Motorsport Manager mobile versus PC version
differences depth review Playsport Games".

Key hits (NBA 2K):

- **Primary (dev blog reprint)** — Operation Sports, "NBA 2K21 Next-Gen MyNBA
  Revealed",
  <https://www.operationsports.com/nba-2k21-next-gen-mynba-revealed-the-new-franchise-mode-combines-mygm-myleague-myleague-online/>:
  MyNBA merges MyGM + MyLEAGUE + MyLEAGUE Online; "Role-Playing Elements"
  (the old MyGM features: conversations, tasks, morale, budgets) become
  **toggles**; former MyLEAGUE players "can now pick and choose"; the split
  had existed because merging "took a tremendous amount of development and
  design work", not as a design ideal.
- NLSC summary, <https://www.nba-live.com/mynba-is-the-new-franchise-mode-in-nba-2k21-next-gen/>:
  RPG elements toggleable; **Ranked** play "limits the amount of
  customisation".
- Operation Sports, "NBA 2K20 MyGM and MyLeague",
  <https://www.operationsports.com/nba-2k20-mygm-and-myleague-the-more-things-change-the-more-they-stay-the-same/>:
  MyGM 2.0's Action-Point / online-leaderboard pivot "stripped away the near
  limitless customization … turned it into more of an arcade-like mode" →
  community disappointment; MyLeague praised as "the standout franchise mode
  among all sports games".
- Operation Sports forum, "MyNBA has lost its way…" (2024-11),
  <https://forums.operationsports.com/forums/forum/basketball/nba-2k-basketball/952201-mynba-has-lost-its-way>:
  2K later re-split MyGM and MyNBA into separate modes; some players welcome
  the split, others mourn the unified toggle model ("you could mix and match
  elements of both modes").
- NBA 2K26 Courtside Report, <https://nba.2k.com/2k26/courtside-report/mynba/>:
  Unranked MyGM allows editing/rosters/sliders; sim speed toggles
  (Normal/Smarter/Faster).

Key hits (Motorsport Manager):

- Motorsport.com interview with Playsport founder Christian West (2016),
  <https://www.motorsport.com/general/news/motorsport-manager-the-game-by-racing-fans-for-racing-fans-740431/740431/>:
  PC version vs mobile is "an entirely different ball game … massively
  enhanced gameplay and unparalleled depth"; mobile "at its heart it's a very
  good strategy game"; PC info "split into chunks so that you're only ever
  thinking about smaller parts of it".
- Autosport review, <https://www.autosport.com/esports/news/motorsport-manager-full-game-review-5108974/5108974/>:
  per-driver driving style + engine mode; setup screen has a **guidance bar**
  for the "less mechanically minded" (assist inside the deep game); three
  race speeds.
- Digitally Downloaded review,
  <https://www.digitallydownloaded.net/2016/11/review-motorsport-manager-pc.html>:
  "complex … lots of strategic decisions … while still being fairly
  accessible to newcomers".
- Strat Packer blog review, <https://stratpack.blog/2021/08/27/motorsport-manager-review>:
  mobile-first 2014, PC 2016; race layer = pace/tyres/fuel/part-condition
  management.

Key hits (Football Chairman Pro — from Query run alongside):

- GameBrain review aggregation, <https://gamebrain.co/game/football-chairman-pro>:
  praised "highly addictive and easy to play"; criticism: "Limited direct
  control over team tactics and player selections; manager often makes
  questionable decisions without chairman input"; "Randomness in match and
  season results can feel unfair".
- iosgames.net review page, <https://www.iosgames.net/Football-Chairman-Pro/index.html>:
  "By stripping away the minute-to-minute gameplay and focusing entirely on
  the executive level…"; positive: "exactly the right depth for a game played
  on the go"; negative user view: "The decisions you make in this game have
  absolutely no impact on your success. You can't organize your squad."
- Google Play listing,
  <https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.undergroundcreative.footballchairmanpro&hl=en_US>:
  feature list (managers use different formations and playing styles; youth
  squad; personalities) — the *manager AI*, not the player, owns tactics.
